Shiro Wot

What exactly is shiro?

Shiro is a popular stape in Ethiopia. It can be prepared from many cereals. It is high in protein. And many use on a daily basis since it doesn't cost much.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Shior flour

Steps

  1. first peel the onion and make sure it it cooked till it is brown
  2. add some water
  3. when the water boils add shiro flour
  4. stir till it is ready
