Genfo

What is Genfo?

Genfo is a popular food in Ethiopia. It is occasionally eaten by women after giving birth. It is belived that it can help recover.

Ingredients

  • Wheat,Barely flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil or Butter
  • Pepper

    • Steps

    1. Boil the Water
    2. Add the Flour
    3. Mix it well until it is cooked well
    4. Add some pepper and butter/oil
    5. Ready to be served
